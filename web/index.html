🎯 How It Works

Problem: Traditional income verification exposes your exact salary to banks, landlords, and employers.

Solution: QS-PID uses zero-knowledge proofs to prove your income exceeds a threshold without revealing the exact amount. Privacy preserved, trust maintained.

💵 Prover (Generate Proof)

Enter your income and the threshold you need to prove against. The system will generate a zero-knowledge proof.

Example: ₹7,50,000 = 750000 INR (7.5 LPA)
Example: ₹5,00,000 = 500000 INR (5 LPA)

✓ Verifier (Verify Proof)

Paste the proof JSON from the prover. The system will verify without learning the exact income.
